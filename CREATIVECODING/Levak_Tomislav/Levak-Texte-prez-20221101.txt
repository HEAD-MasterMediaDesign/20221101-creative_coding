Persistence

Do You Wanna Be Mine; by Dj Rashad, Dj Spinn & Dj Paypal; Courtesy of Teklife

Based on persistence of vision and other optical illusions, this project alternates simple shapes and animations
to trick the eyes of the viewer and their perception of reality.

Try to look at the center of your screen for the duration of the clip and have a look at the room around you!
